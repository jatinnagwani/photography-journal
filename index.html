About / Artist Statement

I’m Jatin, an engineering student at Newton School of Technology, and a photographer who found meaning in stillness. Photography started as curiosity and slowly became purpose. I picked up the camera to remember the small, honest corners; the hush after rain, the angle of morning light, the geometry of empty streets. What began as random frames turned into a way of seeing, a way of listening. Between circuits and classes, I learned that every photograph isn’t about perfection; it’s about presence; a quiet attempt to hold a fleeting moment before it disappears.
